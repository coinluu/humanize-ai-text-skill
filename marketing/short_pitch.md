# Short Pitch

## 10-Second Pitch

Humanize AI Text Skill helps agents rewrite AI-generated Chinese text into natural, platform-ready content while preserving facts.

## 30-Second Pitch

Humanize AI Text Skill is an open-source Agent Skill for improving AI-generated Chinese drafts. It supports AI-tone diagnosis, human-like rewriting, platform adaptation, content type routing, reference sample style extraction, protected facts preservation, safety checks, and anti-overhumanization. It is stateless and does not require a database or backend.

## Developer Pitch

For developers building agents, Humanize AI Text Skill provides a structured prompt-and-rule workflow for Chinese text rewriting. It includes modular prompts, reusable rules, realistic examples, and test specs for safer, more consistent output.

## Content Creator Pitch

For content creators, this skill helps turn stiff AI drafts into more natural Chinese content for Xiaohongshu, Douyin scripts, WeChat articles, Moments, sales copy, and business documents without inventing facts or cases.

## Agent User Pitch

For agent users, this skill gives your agent a clear workflow for rewriting AI text: parse input, protect facts, diagnose AI tone, adapt to platform, rewrite, check consistency, and output a usable version.
